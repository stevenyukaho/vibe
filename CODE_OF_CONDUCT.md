## IBM VIBE code of conduct

IBM VIBE is a place to ask questions, work through challenges, and share ideas and best practices. The following guidelines are intended to make the community easier and more enjoyable to participate in.

### TLDR

- **Be respectful and understanding.** Disagreement is no excuse for poor behavior. Resolve differences constructively.
- **Be welcoming.** We strive to be a community that supports people of all backgrounds and identities.

### Scope

Communication through any of this project’s channels (GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project spaces) must be constructive and must never resort to personal attacks, trolling, public or private harassment, insults, or other unprofessional conduct.

We promise to extend courtesy and respect to everyone involved in this project, regardless of gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, age, race, ethnicity, religion, or level of experience. We expect anyone contributing to IBM VIBE to do the same.

If any member of the community violates this Code of Conduct, the maintainers of IBM VIBE may take action, including removing issues, comments, and pull requests or blocking accounts, as deemed appropriate.

### Reporting

If you are subjected to or witness unacceptable behavior, or have any other concerns, please report it:

- **Preferred (private)**: email `vibe-maintainers@example.com`.

- **Alternative (public)**: open a GitHub issue with the label `code-of-conduct`.
